About the Salon

Queen Street's Favourite Hairdressers

Since opening our doors on Queen Street, Coleraine over 25 years ago, Simon Ferris Hairdressing has built a loyal following of clients who love looking their best — and know exactly where to go to get there.

We're not your average high-street salon. Every member of our senior team brings over a decade of hands-on experience, backed by continuous training in the latest trends and techniques. Whether you want a classic cut or a bold transformation, we make it happen.

Our clients keep coming back — and they keep referring their friends — because we genuinely care about the result every single time you sit in our chair.

What We Offer

Services That Make You Look Incredible

From a fresh trim to a full colour transformation — we've got you covered. Every service delivered with expert care.

Cuts & Styling

From precision cuts to creative styles, our expert stylists shape your hair to complement your face perfectly. Walk out feeling like a new person every time.

Colour & Highlights

Balayage, full colour, highlights, or a subtle refresh — our colourists blend art with technique to give you gorgeous, natural-looking results that turn heads.

Hair Extensions

Want more length or volume? Our professional extension service adds beautiful, seamless volume that blends naturally and lasts — looking stunning from day one.

Blow Dry & Finish

Get that fresh-from-the-salon look whenever you need it. Our blow-dry service leaves your hair smooth, shiny and styled to perfection — ready to walk straight into any room with confidence.

Treatments & Head Massage

Repair, hydrate and restore your hair's natural shine with our professional treatments. Pair it with our legendary head massage — our clients say it's "to die for."

Men's Grooming

Sharp cuts, clean finishes, and styling that keeps you looking your best. Our experienced stylists understand what men want — a great look without the fuss.
